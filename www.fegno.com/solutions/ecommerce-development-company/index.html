Integrations for Agile Business Operations

Inventory management
We help you maintain accurate recordkeeping and replenishment of inventory stored in multiple locations thanks to:
  • Advanced IoT-powered inventory tracking.
  • Demand-driven inventory planning.
  • Rule-based inventory cataloging.
  • Automated purchase orders.
  • Inventory return management.
Order management

We facilitate processing of customer orders generated in different sales channels, distributed via different delivery channels and demanding different packaging or delivery conditions. To achieve this, we ensure:

  • Integration with all the sales channels for centralized order aggregation.
  • Integration with inventory management software.
  • Rule-based order processing.
  • Order routing to an optimal order fulfillment center.
  • Support for different order execution scenarios.
  • Return management.

Logistics management

We help organize timely and cost-effective transportation of goods by enabling:

  • AI-supported planning of shipping routes.
  • Real-time tracking of transportation progress.
  • RFID-enabled monitoring of goods transportation conditions.

Product information management

We eliminate time-stealing repeat work of promoting products in different sales channels. For that, we implement a PIM system with:

  • Product data import.
  • Product catalogization.
  • Rule-based product data enrichment.
  • Product associations: upsells, cross-sells, bundles.
  • PIM integration with product distribution channels.

Digital marketing automation
We give you tools to scale your marketing activities up to a multichannel strategy:

  • Behavioral customer micro-segmentation.
  • Response-driven marketing campaigns.
  • Email marketing.
  • Conversational marketing via live chats, chatbots, and messaging apps.
  • Programmatic advertising.
  • Loyalty programs.
Retail BI

We show you how to use your business data for decision-making via:

  • Data aggregation from heterogeneous internal and external data sources.
  • Multidimensional data analysis.
  • Reporting on chosen KPIs.
  • ML-based forecasting.
